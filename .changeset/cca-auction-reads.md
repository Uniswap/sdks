Add continuous-clearing-auction read descriptors, decoders, and ABIs for live launch data: `clearingPriceCall`/`getClearingPrice` + `decodeCheckpoint` (the auction's `checkpoint()` clearing price, Q96 raw-currency-per-raw-token), the `TICK_DATA_LENS_ABI` with `tickDataCall`/`getTickData` + `decodeInitializedTicks` and the pure `deriveTickFillRatios` (per-tick `currencyDemand / requiredCurrencyDemand` over the lens's live bid-distribution ticks), `currencyRaisedCall`/`remainingSupplyCall`/`isGraduatedCall`/`slot0Call`, `deriveAuctionOutcome`, the `TickDataLens` registry (`getTickDataLensForFactory`), the `CCA_BID_SUBMITTED_EVENT` ABI, and `decodeBidSubmitted` (typed decode of a `BidSubmitted` feed log). Also adds the generic `computeV4PoolId(poolKey)` (canonical v4 `PoolKey.toId()` encoding; `computeLbpPoolId` now sorts-then-delegates to it). The block-latency `Source` factories that stream this data now live in `@uniswap/blockfeed-sdk`.
